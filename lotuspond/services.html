The Design Process

  1. 1

    Post inquiry, We will schedule a chat session to discuss if we would be a suitable match and when I can get you in! I am fine with email correspondance, a Zoom call, or just a normal phone call. I will then send a quote for you to approve! One that is approved, I will send you a contract to sign and an invoice for the 50% non-refundable deposit, and once that has been paid, you are all set!

  2. 2

    To kick off the project, you will receive my brand questionnaire. This helps answer those pressing questions about your brand direction and inspiration for your brand identity!

  3. 3

    From the information provided by the brand questionnaire, and my own research, I will develop strategy documents and a moodboard. After approval, I will then begin developing and prototyping ideas.

  4. 4

    I design and present you with a number of brand concepts, based on your package of choice. Each concept will include all of the branding elements of your selected package.

  5. 5

    Then is refining and tweaking your favorite concept! There will be 2 rounds of revisions until we are confident in the final product.

  6. 6

    I will then begin work on your website if there is one included in your package. There will be 2 rounds of revisions for this, too!

  7. 7

    Finally, I will send over all of the necessary files for you. Some offboarding tips will be included to send you on your merry way! Then you choose how to celebrate your new brand design! Have a cake or something. These things are monumental!

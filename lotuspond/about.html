Hi! I'm Hana.

Let's get to know each other!

Hana (Any Pronouns) is the sole owner and employee behind Lotus Pond Creative. She is a web developer, graphic designer, app developer, as well as a heavy oversharer. She is also terrible at speaking in 3rd person.

I've been deep into the tech world for the greater portion of 10 years now, and there is absolutely nothing that outshines my love for a well-designed website, whether that be a professional level site or an indie site. That's what got me into this line of work!

I started my career doing freelance jobs willy-nilly with the support of my father and family friends, and in 2024, I decided to begin my own design business.

I strive to inspire people like me to explore the world of digital art, as well as to express themselves as authentically as possible, no matter where that might be.

About Me (Concise Edition)

Hi there! I'm 19 years old and I've been exploring the digital art world since 2018!
There is absolutely nothing that outshines my love for a well-designed website, whether that be a professional level site or an indie site. That's what got me into this line of work!
I strive to inspire people like me to explore the world of digital art, as well as to express themselves as authentically as possible, no matter where that might be.
The first impression people get of you offline is your style! Who's to say it's any different online?

(not) Government-mandated fun facts!

I am almost entirely self-taught in both web development and graphic design. I'm working to change that as you read this message.

I'm in the process of learning how to program with Apple's Swift!

I started on the indie-web first. I think it's a wonderful place to start out and meet like-minded people!

I love flowers! My favorites are lotuses, cherry blossoms, hibiscus, chrysanthemums, and wisteria.
